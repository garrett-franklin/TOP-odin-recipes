Jalapeno and Cucumber Cocktail

Description

This jalapeño vodka cocktail features Devotion Vodka, marketed as the world's first sugar-free, gluten-free, and American-made vodka family. This drink recipe is perfect for those who have dietary restrictions. With July 4th celebrations and BBQs in full swing, adults 21 and over can treat themselves to a refreshing version of their favorite specialty cocktails.

Ingredients

  • 1 fluid ounce cucumber-flavored syrup
  • 2 slices jalapeno pepper
  • ice cubes
  • 4.5 fluid ounces club soda
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
  • 1 splash lime juice
  • 1 cucumber slice

Steps

  1. Muddle cucumber-flavored syrup and jalapeño pepper together in a cocktail shaker. Fill the shaker with ice and pour in club soda, vodka, and lime juice. Cover shaker and shake until the outside of the shaker becomes frosted.
  2. Pour the cocktail into a glass and garnish with a cucumber slice.
