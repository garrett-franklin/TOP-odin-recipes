Easy Pina Colada

Description

Work got you down? Kids fighting? Kick back and relax with this delicious tropical beverage, guaranteed to transport you away from your troubles to a private beach somewhere warm.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1 can (7 ounce) sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • 7 fluid ounces white rum
  • 2 cups ice

Steps

  1. Combine pineapple, coconut milk, rum, and ice in a blender and puree until smooth and creamy. Serve immediately.
