Mind Eraser

Description

After a long day, sometimes you just need to forget. Fear not, this beverage will help you leave your troubles behind almost immediately. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua)
  • 1/4 cup lemond-lime soda (such as Sprite)
  • 1/4 cup vodka

Steps

  1. Gently layer the coffee-flavored liqueur, lemon-lime soda, and vodka into either a shot glass or a cocktail glass filled with ice. This is a layered drink so pour them in the order listed and do not mix the layers before drinking! Drink with a straw until all layers are gone.
